1) How the outputs of Field Image is choosen?

Every Single box represents a 4.5 to 4.5 cm square on the actual game field, there are 72 identical boxes around the field.

2) I can't use the excel on my macbook, what Can I do?

Simple, you can always ask for the back up excel file from your scout leader.

3) How do I scout with this website?

There are six 6 robots per match in the field. Every scouter should pick one of the robots and record their data to website by answering self explanatory questions in each step (pre match, auto, etc.). After that every scouter should reach out to their scout leader and send the qr code to them.
